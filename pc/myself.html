My story

I started as a Graphic Designer & Journalist Photographer in 2013 and thanks to many years of traveling, working with so many people, I have found out that the core of successful design work is concern about what my audience's thoughts are.

Design should be a tool to convey a message, deliver a value to satisfy the right problem that the audience is facing. Knowing exactly what they needs, combined with the existing skills, can produce results beyond the expectations.

Being aware of that led me to dig deeper and deeper into the field and become an UX/UI designer and somehow, now a digital product designer. But no matter what title I work under, what I always put first, is to solve the user's problem.
