I was not born and bred in Hanoi, but spent most of my time studying and working here, long enough to say I understand and love this city. Seeing the city that is so familiar through the lens of the camera gives me more experiences and new perspectives on where I live.

I love to spend my freetime strolling around the city to see the people's life, to capture the development and to grasp the sharp contrast between rich and poor or simply just find the happy moments among the rat race life.
